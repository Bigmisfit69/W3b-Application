About the Community Centre:

The Cambridge Community Center (CCC), established in 1929, is a nonprofit organization located in Cambridge, Massachusetts. It serves the youth, families, and seniors of the Riverside community by offering a variety of social, cultural, educational, and recreational programs. These initiatives are designed to promote community cooperation, unity, and empowerment. The center's facilities include a 5,500 sq. ft. gymnasium, a 900 sq. ft. common room, classrooms, and a computer lab, all available for community use and events. Situated between Central Square and Memorial Drive, the CCC is easily accessible and continues to be a vital resource for the Cambridge community.
